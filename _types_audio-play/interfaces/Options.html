Various options for audio playback

param

The timestamp at which to start the audio. Can be negative to start from end. (Default: 0)

param

The timestamp the audio ends at. (Default: length of audio buffer)

param

Plays back the audio immediately upon loading. (Default: false)

param

Continuously loops the buffer until paused. (Default: false)

param

Handle to an audio context. If not provided, one is provided for you.
